HMM! everyone - audio books

For the Macro Monday theme "Relaxation" (May 5th 2014)

I have to rest up a whole lot, and my main way to relax and enjoy it is listening to an audio book. The luxury of having someone read to you. Delicious ;o)

Shot with the Sony NEX-6 using the Lensbaby Composer Pro with 4+ macro filter and the F2.8 aperture ring

See the whole of my Macro Monday set here: www.flickr.com/photos/e_liddell/sets/72157633385030336/
